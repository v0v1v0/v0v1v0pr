Preflect R Documentation

Reflect a pole through to the lower hemisphere

Description

Takes a vector to a pole and reflects it to the lower hemisphere

Usage

Preflect(az, dip)

Arguments

az

azimuth angle, degrees

dip

dip in degrees

Value

list

azimuth angle, degrees

dip in degrees
