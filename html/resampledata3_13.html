Eyes R Documentation

Eyes

Description

Measurements of eyes of 40 people.

Usage

data("Eyes")

Format

A data frame with 40 observations on the following 6 variables.

ID

Subject ID

age

Age of subject

hand

Dominant hand of subject, left or right

eye

Dominant eye of subject, left or right

leftPD

Left pupillary distance (mm)

rightPD

Right pupillary distance (mm)

Source

Westfield (private communication).
