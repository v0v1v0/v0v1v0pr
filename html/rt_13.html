rt_logout R Documentation

Log out of RT

Description

Use this to log out of RT at the end of your session. Note: restarting your R session will also log you out.

Usage

rt_logout(...)

Arguments

...

Other arguments passed to rt_POST

Value

(rt_api) The parsed response from RT

Examples

## Not run: 
# First, log in
rt_login()

# Then logout
rt_logout()

## End(Not run)
