raceland: Pattern-Based Zoneless Method for Analysis and Visualization of Racial Topography

Description

Implements a computational framework for a pattern-based, zoneless analysis, and visualization of (ethno)racial topography (Dmowska, Stepinski, and Nowosad (2020) doi:10.1016/j.apgeog.2020.102239).
