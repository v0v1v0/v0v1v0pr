VILL R Documentation

Villarrica DEM

Description

Digital Elevation Model (DEM) of Villarrica Volcano, Chile.

Format

List with following elements:

x

Easting from vent (m)

y

Northing from vent (m)

z

Elevation above sea level (m)

lat

Latitude (degrees)

lon

Longitude (degrees)

Examples

data(VILL)

contour(VILL)
