ChiMarathonMen R Documentation

ChiMarathonMen

Description

Times from a sample of men who completed the Chicago marathon in 2015.

Usage

data("ChiMarathonMen")

Format

A data frame with 80 observations on the following 4 variables.

name

Name of competitor

Division

Age group

Finish

Finish time

FinishMin

Time in minutes

Source

https://chicago-history.r.mikatiming.com/
