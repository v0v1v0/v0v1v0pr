Girls2004 R Documentation

Births of girls in Alaska or Wyoming

Description

Data on births of a random sample of girls in Alaska or Wyoming in 2004.

Usage

data("Girls2004")

Format

A data frame with 80 observations on the following 6 variables.

ID

Subject ID

State

State: AK or WY

MothersAge

Age of mother: 15-19, 20-24, 25-29, 30-34, 35-39, 40-44

Smoker

Mother a smoker? No or Yes

Weight

Weight of baby (grams)

Gestation

Gestation time (weeks)

Source

http://wonder.cdc.gov/natality-current.html
