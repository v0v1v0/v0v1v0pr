roadoi_addin R Documentation

Find OA copies with RStudio addin

Description

An RStudio addin to call oadoi_fetch(). Shows up as "Find free full-texts" in the RStudio addin menu.

Usage

roadoi_addin()

Details

The addin works as follows:

  1. Copy up to ten line-separated DOIs into the text area

  2. Press the button "Run!"

  3. Click on the links in the table to download full-text
