rmtfit.object R Documentation

Estimated restricted mean times in favor of treatment

Description

This class of objects is returned by the rmtfit class of functions. Objects of this class have methods for the functions print, summary, plot, and bouquet.

Value

t

A vector of follow-up times \tau.

mu

A matrix with K+2 rows; The kth row (k=1,\ldots, K) is \mu_k(\tau), the restricted mean time in favor of treatment on the kth state (or recurrent event); The (K+1)th row is the net restricted mean survival time; The last row is the overal effect \mu(\tau).
var

A matrix with K+2 rows containing the variance estimates for mu.

mu10, mu01

Matrices with K+1 rows; The kth row (k=1,\ldots, K) is the restricted mean win (mu10) and loss (mu01) times by the treatment on the kth state (or recurrent event); The (K+1)th row is that on the survival time.
