rorq_massratio R Documentation

Rorqual engulfed water mass to body mass ratio

Description

Rorqual engulfed water mass to body mass ratio

Usage

rorq_massratio(species, length_m)

Arguments

species

a vector of species codes
length_m

a vector of lengths in meters

Value

a vector of ratios (engulfed water mass to body mass)

Examples

# A 22m blue whale
rorq_massratio("bw", 22)

# A 7m minke
rorq_massratio("ba", 7)
